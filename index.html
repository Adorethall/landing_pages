FAQ

Common Questions from Roblox Devs

Does Meshy export formats that work in Roblox Studio? +
Yes. Meshy exports .OBJ and .FBX — both of which Roblox Studio accepts natively. You can import them directly without any conversion tools or plugins.
Will the polygon count exceed Roblox's limits and cause lag? +
Meshy's generation pipeline is tuned for game-engine use. Assets come out with reasonable polygon counts by default, and you can request even lighter "low-poly" variants specifically optimized for Roblox performance budgets.
Can Meshy match the cartoon / blocky Roblox aesthetic? +
Absolutely. Include style keywords in your prompt — "low poly", "cartoon", "blocky", "stylized" — and Meshy will generate assets that fit naturally alongside Roblox's visual style.
Is there a free tier? Do I need a credit card? +
Meshy offers a free tier with no credit card required. You can generate and download assets immediately after signing up. Paid plans unlock higher resolution, faster generation, and commercial use rights.
Can I use generated assets in monetized Roblox games? +
Yes. Paid plan subscribers receive full commercial rights to all generated assets, including use in Robux-monetized games and games sold on Roblox marketplace.
